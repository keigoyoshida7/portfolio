Tsukuba International Conference 2023 session performance


Performance Notes:
I. Drum Solo Performance.
II. Audio Visual × Drum Performance.
III. AI Shinya Fujii music × Drum session.

II. Max and Jitter were used to conduct an audio-reactive visual performance.
III. Hifi-GAN, an AI model, was utilized to transform and synthesize the vocal melody of an original composition into the vocals of Dr. Shinya Fujii, creating a new musical piece.
By incorporating the generated musical piece, a session was conducted involving human drum performance, creating a session between human drumming and the AI-generated vocal composition.

Performer
